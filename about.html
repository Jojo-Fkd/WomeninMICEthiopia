Who We Are

Women in MICE Ethiopia is board led, non – profit making, non – religious, non-political and non – governmental women’s organization with special interest in empowerment of girls and women to contribute in transforming the society in positively and impacting a positive change. WIM Ethiopia is mainly involved in empowering Ethiopian women and girls, aiming for their personal, career, and economic success through empowerment and by mobilizing women’s participation in securing occupation, promotion of their products and services to the public, through training and skills development activities through coaching and mentorship.

Mission

Promote the empowerment of women and girls at all levels to develop their natural abilities to their fullest potential.
Equip Ethiopian women and girls with skills and knowledge to excel as leaders of their workplaces and communities in spheres of the economic, social, political and legal empowerment. 
Enhance collaboration and synergy with different human & women rights advocate organizations towards gender equality and an enhanced level of engagement of women in Ethiopia.

Human Rights & Protection

The women's and girls' rights, the sexual reproductive and health rights, protection of women and girls from all kinds of violence, gender equality, the laws and legal frame works issues etc Are going to be considered in this country program.
